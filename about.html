About Travelr

In 2011, Travelr was born out of a never ending online search for a trip with a difference. We spent hours trawling, trying to weigh up the results on one tab against another. There must be an easier way of doing this, we thought. Surely there's a travel group that operate out of the most beautiful places on the planet, while also offering cool adventures and new experiences.
But there wasn't. So we started Travelr.


Our company comprises a small group of friends who share a love of travel. We promise to have personally tested each of our holiday packages, and if it was less than perfect - it wouldn't be on our site.
Trust our judgement? Take the quiz and we can tell you where to head next.
